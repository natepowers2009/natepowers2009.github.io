Mcdonald's Garfield Promotion

In 1978, Mcdonald's promoted garfield merchandise by releasing a mug, which was originally intended to contain a comic to bring a smile to people's faces, but instead ended the lives of many as people as it was soon discovered to contain 169,000 PPM of lead, and 93,300 PPM of cadmium. Lead can cause brain damage, and is illegal in amounts past 90 PPM, which is dangerous for kids.
